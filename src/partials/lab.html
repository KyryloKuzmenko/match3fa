How Matching Works in Deep Space

You'll never match alone. Your crewmates boost your power — each with their own flavor.

  • Tiles fall in orbital spiral, not grid

  • Events: Frozen Tiles, Asteroid Fog, Solar Flare Turns

  • Combos create wormholes → explode rows

  • Boosters: Gravity Bombs, Plasma Tornado, Glitch-Wipe

  • "Logic Snap" — chain 6+ tiles, freeze time
