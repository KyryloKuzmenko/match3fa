• Can I play offline?

    Yes! Space Matchers is fully optimized for offline gameplay. Whether you're in airplane mode or lost in a Wi-Fi dead zone, all 200+ levels are accessible. Your progress is saved locally and syncs with your account when you reconnect to the internet. It's a perfect game for flights, commuting, or relaxing on the couch with no distractions.

  • What makes Space Matchers different from other match-3 games?

    Space Matchers combines traditional tile-matching mechanics with spatial physics and a character-based synergy system. Instead of repetitive level grids, you get orbital paths, rotating boards, and evolving environmental hazards. On top of that, the game features no ads, no timers, and no forced purchases. All characters are unlocked through in-game progress—not payments.

  • What happens if I fail a level?

    Failure doesn't block your journey—it changes it. When a level is failed, the galaxy enters a state of minor distortion: tiles might fall in reversed gravity, warp speed increases, or you might lose access to certain boosters temporarily. But don't worry — these shifts reset when you replay or complete levels elsewhere. It encourages adaptation and adds variety.

  • How do the space animals help in gameplay?

    Each crewmate you unlock brings a special passive power. For example, Catryna boosts diagonals, while Lamirez randomly clones power tiles. You can customize your active crew before each level, creating synergy between character powers and booster loadouts. This transforms traditional match-3 into a more tactical experience where your team composition directly impacts the outcome.

  • Is the game really free? Where's the catch?

    The game is 100% free to play and free of disruptive ads. Monetization is cosmetic only — for example, buying a new visual skin for your tiles, or unlocking animated finishers for boosters. There are no paywalls, no energy limits, and no levels that require spending to pass. You earn rewards through skill and consistency, not your wallet.

  • Will there be more content in the future?

    Absolutely. The game is designed to expand. New galaxies, crewmates, tile types, and space events are introduced monthly. Players who finish the current campaign can access event galaxies, seasonal quests, and "Void Challenges" — ultra-hard puzzle chains with exclusive visuals and titles.
